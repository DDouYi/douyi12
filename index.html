Yi Dou

Postdoctoral fellow in PolyU (Email: douyi12 AT gmail.com)

I am a postdoctoral fellow in Department of Computing, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, supervised by Dr. Allen M.H. Au.

I received my Ph.D. degree from Department of Computing, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, supervised by Dr. Henry C. B. Chan.

I received my Bachelor and Master degrees from Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications in 2011 and 2014, respectively, supervised by Dr. Haiping Huang.

I worked as a software engineer in ZTE corporation in 2014.

My general research interests are in the areas of distributed systems and privacy-enhancing technologies.


You can find more information about me in CV, Google scholar, DBLP.
